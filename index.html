The Top 10 Songs of

2019...according to me!


15. ME!

Taylor Swift ft. Panic! At The Disco's Brendon Urie

Panic!At The Disco's Brendon Urie collaborated with Taylor Swift on this adorable,funky song about true love. It's made its way onto my playlist for when I feel sad,to get me hyped up again!The visuals in the music video are ethereal, with glowing lights and flowing dresses (literally, you can watch for yourself!)

14. On My Way

Alan Walker ft. Sabrina Carpenter

What a beautiful composition! This song is about a girl on her way to prove herself and create a pathway to success for herself. When I need uplifting, I listen to this song.

13. Kill This Love

BLACKPINK

Going through some tough times in your life? Just listen to this song! I love it because of the catchy beats and the bold visuals. Ironically, I'm not killing my love for this song any time soon!

12. Without Me

Halsey

This song really hits home. Halsey sings about helping someone up from a bad place, only to be forgotten by them and left heartbroken. Just give the song a listen, it's so meaningful.

11. 7 rings

Ariana Grande

This is actually one of the most catchy songs I've heard(believe me,I caught myself humming it in class at least 100 times). Best recommened to listen to with your squad!

10. bury a friend

Billie Eilish

Ok, I can't help the fact that this eerily enchanting song made its way onto my top 10 songs list. Honestly, that's all I need to say to get you listening (hopefully).
