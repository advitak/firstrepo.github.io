15. ME!

Taylor Swift and Brendon Urie of Panic!At The Disco

Panic!At The Disco's Brendon Urie collaborated with Taylor Swift on this adorable,
funky song about true love. It's made its way onto my playlist
for when I feel sad, to get me hyped up again!
The visuals in the music video are ethereal, with glowing lights and flowing dresses (literally, you can watch for yourself!)
